Chicken Curry

Chicken curry is common to several countries including India, countries in Asia and the Caribbean.

Published September 24, 2023 by Chef MeowMeow

Chicken curry is a beloved and flavorful dish that originates from various culinary traditions around the world. It typically consists of tender pieces of chicken simmered in a rich and aromatic sauce made with a blend of spices, herbs, and often, creamy coconut milk or tomato-based gravy.
The result is a mouthwatering combination of savory, spicy, and slightly sweet flavors that make chicken curry a true comfort food.
Whether you prefer it mild or fiery hot, chicken curry is a versatile dish that can be customized to suit your taste buds. Serve it with rice or naan bread for a satisfying and heartwarming meal that's perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Coriander, cumin, turmeric, fennel seeds, cinnamon, pepper, ground mustard, ground cloves, cayenne pepper and salt - these spices build incredible flavor in this dish. Together they create a homemade curry blend.
  • Olive oil - used for sautéing.
  • Fresh onion, garlic and ginger - these aromatics add a key depth of flavor.
  • Low-sodium chicken broth - to create a sauce base to coat the chicken.
  • Canned tomatoes - fresh tomatoes will work here too but canned are quicker and easier.
  • Chicken breasts - be careful not to over-cook so the chicken doesn’t become dry.
  • Cornstarch - used to thicken the sauce slightly.
  • Heavy cream - this makes the sauce deliciously rich and creamy.
  • Chopped cilantro - adds a nice prop of fresh flavor and color.

Instructions

  1. Mix spices together
  2. Saute onion, garlic and ginger in a skillet.
  3. Add broth and tomatoes, simmer.
  4. Blend mixture in a blender as directed then return to skillet.
  5. Season sauce then add chicken.
  6. Simmer chicken in sauce until cooked through.
  7. Add cornstarch slurry during last minute to thicken sauce.
  8. Stir in cream.
  9. Serve over rice garnished with cilantro.

Cook Time

20 Minutes
