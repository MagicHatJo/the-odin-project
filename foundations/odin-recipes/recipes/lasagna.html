Meat Lasagna

This timeless classic features sweet Italian sausage, lean ground beef, and 3 types of cheese for a decadent meal.

Published September 24, 2023 by Chef MeowMeow

Lasagna is a classic, comforting dish that everyone loves. With two types of meat, a tangy tomato sauce, and three types of cheese, it's sure to be a hit at any dinner or potluck.
The best part of this recipe is that it freezes and reheats well, making it the perfect dish to prepare ahead of time and enjoy throughout the week. Pair it with my garlic knots or garlic bread recipe for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage - use spicy or mild Italian sausage based on your preference.
  • Ground Beef - lean ground beef works best for this recipe.
  • Onion and Garlic - using fresh onion and garlic make the sauce so much more flavorful.
  • Tomatoes - tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crush tomatoes blend together to create a tangy, flavorful sauce base.
  • Sugar - sugar enhances the flavor of the sauce by adding a subtle sweetness to it.
  • Spices and Seasonings - herbs give your sauce depth by in fusing it with a bit of earthiness.
  • Lasagna Noodles - store-bought and homemade noodles both work wonderfully for this recipe.
  • Cheese - using parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta together make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg - egg helps bind the ricotta together so that it won't be runny when you cut into the lasagna.

Instructions

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the layering instructions below.
  5. Cover the lasagna tray with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

Layering

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat until all ingredients are used up
