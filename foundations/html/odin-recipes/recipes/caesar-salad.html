Caesar Salad

A classic that makes for a great appetizer, side dish, or crowd pleaser at a potluck.

Published September 24, 2023 by Chef MeowMeow

Caesar salad is a classic and timeless favorite among salad enthusiasts. It features crisp romaine lettuce leaves tossed in a creamy Caesar dressing, typically made with ingredients like anchovies, garlic, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil, creating a rich and savory flavor profile. To add a satisfying crunch and contrast, the salad is often garnished with croutons and sprinkled with additional grated Parmesan cheese, making it a delightful blend of textures and tastes.

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce - it's a salad.
  • Parmesan Cheese - shredded or shaved.
  • Crisp Croutons - homemade can be prepped in advance.
  • Dressing - homemade is best.
  • Chicken Breasts - MEAATTTT.

Dressing

  1. Whisk together minced garlic, dijon, Worcestershire, lemon juice and red wine vinegar.
  2. Whisking while adding oil emulsifies the dressing for a smooth and creamy (not oily) consistency.
  3. Season with 1/2 tsp salt and 1/8 tsp black pepper, or to taste.
